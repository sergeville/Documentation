# Canadian AI Grants & Funding Reference
<!-- Created: 2026-03-12 | Review: Quarterly -->

## Overview

Reference guide for Canadian funding programs relevant to AI tools, HVAC diagnostics, home automation, and medical software. Prioritized by fit with current projects (Alfred, HVAC agent, VWA, AI Venture Studio).

---

## Tier 1 — Highest Priority

### SR&ED — Scientific Research & Experimental Development
**Type:** Tax credit (refundable for CCPC)
**Amount:** 35% refundable on up to $3M in eligible expenditures (CCPC rate); 15% non-refundable for larger corps
**Timeline:** Claim with T2 annual return; retroactive up to 18 months
**Eligible work:** AI model development, algorithm research, novel integrations (e.g., HVAC + ML inference)
**Fit:** 🔴 Highest — nearly all R&D coding qualifies
**Key rule:** Must demonstrate *technological uncertainty* — you had to experiment to find the answer
**Action:** Track time and document hypotheses/experiments per project. Use HVAC agent dev logs as evidence.
**URL:** https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/scientific-research-experimental-development-tax-incentive-program.html

---

### NRC-IRAP — Industrial Research Assistance Program
**Type:** Non-repayable contribution
**Amount:** $75K–$500K+ (AI Assist stream: up to $200K for first-time applicants)
**Timeline:** Rolling intake; 4–8 week assessment
**Eligible work:** Developing AI products/tools for commercial market; hiring technical staff
**Fit:** 🔴 Very high — designed exactly for this stage (prototype → product)
**Key rule:** Must have <500 employees and be incorporated
**Note:** Assign an Industrial Technology Advisor (ITA) — they are free advisors, not just gatekeepers
**Action:** Contact NRC-IRAP to get assigned an ITA. Prepare a 2-page project brief per project (HVAC, VWA, Alfred).
**URL:** https://nrc.canada.ca/en/support-technology-innovation/nrc-irap

---

## Tier 2 — Medium Priority

### CanExport Innovation
**Type:** Non-repayable grant
**Amount:** Up to $37,500 per project (75% of eligible costs)
**Timeline:** 4 intake windows per year
**Eligible costs:** International market research, IP protection abroad, foreign regulatory approvals
**Fit:** 🟡 Moderate — relevant when ready to export (VWA or HVAC agent to US/EU market)
**Key rule:** Company must be incorporated, <250 employees
**Action:** Bookmark for when HVAC or medical tool approaches product-market fit and targets export.
**URL:** https://www.tradecommissioner.gc.ca/funding-financement/canexport/innovation/index.aspx

---

### NSERC Alliance + Mitacs Accelerate
**Type:** Grants (partnership required)
**Amount:** $7,500 cash contribution → unlocks $22,500 in subsidized grad student work (Mitacs)
**Timeline:** Quarterly intake (Mitacs); NSERC rolling
**Eligible work:** Applied research with a university partner
**Fit:** 🟡 Moderate — good for HVAC domain ML research or medical diagnostic models
**Key rule:** Requires a partnering professor and enrolled grad student
**Action:** Identify a prof at École Polytechnique, Concordia, or McGill working on HVAC/IoT/medical AI.
**Mitacs URL:** https://www.mitacs.ca/en/programs/accelerate
**NSERC Alliance URL:** https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Innovate-Innover/alliance-alliance/index_eng.asp

## Tier 3 — Quebec-Specific (High if Based in QC)

### CDAEIA — Crédit pour le développement des affaires électroniques et des affaires informatiques
**Type:** Quebec tax credit
**Amount:** 30% of eligible AI/tech salaries (stacks on top of SR&ED)
**Administered by:** Revenu Québec
**Fit:** 🔴 Very high if you incorporate in QC — stacks on SR&ED for massive effective rate
**Key rule:** Must be incorporated in QC, dedicated R&D activities
**Action:** Consult a Quebec SR&ED specialist — combining CDAEIA + SR&ED is a significant multiplier.

---

### Investissement Québec — ESSOR / APEX
**Type:** Repayable loans + some grants
**Amount:** Varies ($50K–$1M+)
**Fit:** 🟡 Medium — better for scaling than early R&D
**URL:** https://www.investissement-quebec.com/en/financial-products/

---

## Tier 4 — Lower Priority / Watch List

### CDAP — Canada Digital Adoption Program
**Status:** ⛔ CLOSED — no longer accepting applications (as of 2025)

---

### SCALE AI
**Type:** Collaborative project funding
**Amount:** $1M+ minimum project size
**Fit:** 🔴 Low near-term — requires large consortium or industry partner
**Watch for:** Smaller industry calls may be published
**URL:** https://www.scaleai.ca/en/

---

### BDC — Business Development Bank
**Type:** Loans (not grants)
**Notable:** Data-to-AI Program — up to $250K in advisory + tech loans
**Fit:** 🟡 Consider after proof-of-revenue
**URL:** https://www.bdc.ca/en/articles-tools/technology/implement-new-technologies/bdc-data-ai-program

---

### Ontario OCI — Ontario Centres of Innovation
**Type:** Vouchers + matching grants
**Amount:** $20K–$100K
**Fit:** 🟡 If incorporated in Ontario
**URL:** https://www.oc-innovation.ca/

## Immediate Action Items

1. **SR&ED (this year):** Start tracking engineering time now. Timesheets + experiment logs per project. Consult an SR&ED firm (contingency fee, ~15-20% of credit — no upfront cost).
2. **NRC-IRAP:** Book a call to get assigned an ITA. Prepare a 2-page project brief for HVAC agent or medical diagnostic tool.
3. **CDAEIA (if QC):** Confirm incorporation province and consult Revenu Québec resources — this is high leverage.
4. **CanExport:** Defer until export-ready (likely 12–18 months out).
